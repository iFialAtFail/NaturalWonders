Program Philosophy

Natural Wonders Childcare Center Philosophy: Natural Wonders Childcare Center is a nature-based learning center where children learn through their interactions with the world around them in a safe, secure environment. The children’s learning environment is filled with developmentally appropriate lessons, centered-around the children’s interest and the unique way each child learns. It is the goal of our program to engage parents and create trusting relationships that foster open communication about every child’s education and future. Natural Wonders Childcare Center, Where learning comes naturally!
