The Team

Natural Wonders Childcare Center staff have had an innovative mixture of degrees from area colleges ranging from Early Childhood Education to Elementary Education to Childhood Psychology! We take pride in our education as well as furthering our education with 24 hours of yearly professional development training hours which is 8 hours above the state licensing mandation. Our team is Adult, Child & Infant CPR & First aide trained, Blood Borne Pathogen Trained, Shaken Baby Syndrome Trained, & Infant Safe Sleep Trained!

About the Director

Tamara LeVeque graduated from Morley Stanwood Community Schools in 2006 and graduated from Ferris State University with a Bachelor's Degree in Early Childhood Education in May 2012. Tamara has a passion for children which shows throughout her day as a teacher in the Preschool room at Natural Wonders Childcare Center as well as being the Director. She is also passionate about a natural curriculum and teaching children about the world around them as a basis for their early educational experience! Tamara places a large emphasis on family-school communication because a team effort is of vital importance for the success our our youth!
